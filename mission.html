Our Mission & Vision

Mission

  • Promote and protect every Nigerian child's rights
  • Advocate for policy reforms that benefit children
  • Empower communities to protect their children
  • Provide emergency assistance to vulnerable children

Vision

A Nigeria where every child's rights are respected, protected, and fulfilled. We envision a society where children grow up in safe environments, receive quality education, have access to healthcare, and are free from exploitation and abuse.
