Our Programs

We run impactful programs that protect children's rights and improve their lives across Nigeria.

Child Protection Units

Establishing and supporting local units to identify and respond to child protection cases in communities.

Education Access Initiative

Ensuring out-of-school children gain access to quality education through scholarships, school construction, and teacher training.

Health for All Children

Mobilizing resources to provide essential healthcare services to underserved communities through mobile clinics and partnerships.

Legal Aid & Advocacy

Providing free legal representation and advocacy support for children facing abuse, neglect, or exploitation.

Emergency Response Units

Rapid response teams deployed to assist children affected by emergencies such as natural disasters or conflict zones.

Youth Empowerment Projects

Training and mentorship programs for youth to become advocates and future leaders in child rights.
